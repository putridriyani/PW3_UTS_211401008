THE SCRIPT TIMELINE

  • October 3, 1979: Daniel John Mark Luke O'Donoghue is born.
  • October 29, 1976: Mark Anthony Sheehan is born.
  • July 5, 1980: Glen Joseph Power is born.
  • The Script: Irish pop rock band The Script has released six studio albums, four EPs, 22 singles and 20 music videos. The Script released their debut self-titled studio album, The Script, in 2008. The album went to number one in both Ireland and the United Kingdom. It also reached the top ten in Australia and Sweden, and peaked at number 19 on the US Top Heatseekers chart.
  • August 11, 2008: The Script is released.
  • September 13, 2010: Science & Faith is released.
  • September, 2012: #3 is released.
  • August 27, 2021: I WANT IT ALL – NEW SINGLE IS OUT. Did ye miss us?! Feels amazing to be back with a brand new song for you!! 'I Want It All' is out now everywhere.
  • August 27, 2021: GREATEST HITS TOUR 2022: UK & IRELAND PRE-SALE ACCESS. UK & Ireland! Get pre-sale access to the UK & Irish dates on our Greatest Hits Tour 2022 with Ella Henderson next year.
  • August 27, 2021: TALES FROM THE SCRIPT: GREATEST HITS TOUR & ALBUM. We are beyond excited to tell you all that we’re heading back out on tour in 2022, and our album 'Tales From The Script: The Greatest Hits' will be out on October 1st!
  • September 16, 2021: I WANT IT ALL – OFFICIAL VIDEO IS OUT. We had such a great time going down memory lane for this one... we hope you like it as much as we do.
  • October 1, 2021: TALES FROM THE SCRIPT. IS OUT. This is our story so far and an opportunity for us to celebrate with The Script Family!
  • October 8, 2021: TALES FROM THE SCRIPT. NO.1 ALBUM IN THE UK & IRELAND.
  • January 24, 2022: GREATEST HITS TOUR 2022 EUROPE UPDATE. We’re very disappointed to announce that we will be postponing the European leg of our Greatest Hits Tour to November 2022.
  • March 24, 2022: TALES FROM THE SCRIPT ON GREEN VINYL! is released. It will be released on March 11th, exclusively in good ol' green.
